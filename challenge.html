CppHackGame CTF Challenge

The Game

The CppHackGame is a very small text-based RPG game.
However, this game is designed to be hacked as a challenge.

Once the mechanics reveal themselves, it is possible to beat the game.

You can download the program here:

  • Executable: release_1.0/cpphackgame.exe
  • PDB files: release_1.0/cpphackgame.pdb

Challenge

Can you figure out how much gold you will have if you have completed the game?
